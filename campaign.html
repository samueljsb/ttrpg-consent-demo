All topics are graded according to the lowest consent level of all players.

  • ✅ allowed: this can come up at any time
  • ⚠️ warn first: let everyone know before this comes up
  • 🫣 no interaction: this can happen, but should not involve the players directly
  • 🚪 behind closed doors: this can be alluded to
  • 🚫 not in play: this topic should not feature in this campaign
